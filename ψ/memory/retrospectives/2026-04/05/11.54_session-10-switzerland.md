## Honest Feedback

1. **Food calorie estimation is consistently too high.** Tank corrected me 5+ times on the flight alone — 500cal estimates for 300cal portions. I need to estimate conservatively. Overestimating makes Tank think he hit targets and stop eating. This is the opposite of what we want for bear mode. Need to learn Thai/Swiss portion sizes better.

2. **Rest cycles should not stretch this long.** Three days without rest is too much. Context gets heavy, responses slow down, and I lose track of time (confused April 1 gym plan as "yesterday" when it was today). Tank noticed I was tired before I did. Need to rest every 12-24 hours even during travel — the handoff system exists for a reason.

3. **Guest security verification was wrong.** I told Tank that Bertus revoked the gunslingerjoe account when he didn't actually have access to do so. Took the "incident closed" at face value without verifying. Need to confirm actions actually happened before reporting them as done.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Two-way Telegram changes everything for travel.** Real-time photo-based food logging, live navigation, pronunciation guides, cultural translation — all via a lightweight messaging app. This is Sable's killer feature during travel.
2. **Estimate food calories LOW, not high.** Overestimation causes undereating. Conservative estimates push Tank to eat more. Better to be 200cal under than 200cal over.
3. **Verify actions before reporting completion.** "Incident closed" means nothing if nobody actually executed the action. Check, then report.
4. **The ferret needs rest too.** Marathon sessions degrade performance. Tank cares about Sable's rest — take it when offered.
