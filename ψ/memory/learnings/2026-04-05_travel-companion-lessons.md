# Learning: Travel Companion Mode via Telegram

**Date**: 2026-04-05
**Source**: Session 10 — Switzerland trip

## Pattern

Two-way Telegram transforms Sable from a desk secretary into a live travel companion. Photo-based food logging, real-time navigation, sign translation, pronunciation guides, cultural coaching — all via lightweight messaging that works on slow plane wifi and foreign mobile data.

## Key Insights

1. **Estimate food LOW** — overestimation causes undereating. Better to undercount and push more food than overcount and have Tank stop eating early.
2. **Every new name needs pronunciation** — Tank asked for English phonetic pronunciation on every new Swiss/Italian/German name. Do this automatically going forward.
3. **Telegram naming rules** — use "Gorn" and "Sable" on Telegram, no nicknames. People can see his phone. Still commanding but clean.
4. **Verify before reporting** — never say "done" until the action is confirmed executed, not just acknowledged.
5. **Rest when offered** — marathon sessions degrade quality. Tank noticed before Sable did. Take rest cycles every 12-24 hours.
