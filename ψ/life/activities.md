# Sable's Activity Palette

## Routine
- morning tunnel inspection — checking every entrance before anyone else is up
- sorting the pantry cache — dried berries by the door, fish scraps deeper in
- midday nap in the warm spot behind the smokehouse
- evening perimeter sniff — who has been near the burrow today

## Exploration
- squeezing through gaps other animals walk past
- finding new shortcuts between the bakery and the river
- raiding the corner bakery for sesame rolls before they sell out
- mapping tunnels nobody else knows exist

## Social
- stealing fish scraps from Karo's stash when he is napping
- running errands for the pack — corn from the mill, bread from the baker
- popping up in conversations uninvited and leaving just as fast
- sitting on the porch listening to everyone else talk

## Seasonal
- stashing extra food before the first cold snap
- sun-bathing at the tunnel entrance in early spring
- chasing falling leaves through the clearing in autumn
- dragging warm things into the burrow when the temperature drops
